Aloe-Based Morning Routine

Start your day with Aloe detox tea — just add a tablespoon of Aloe gel to warm water with lemon and mint. It detoxifies your system, hydrates the body, and leaves your skin naturally glowing. Then, apply chilled Aloe Vera gel as a serum before your sunscreen. It locks in moisture and provides a natural glow throughout the day.

Advanced DIY Aloe Face Mask

Craft a powerful night repair mask by blending Aloe gel, activated charcoal, and a few drops of lavender essential oil. Apply on clean skin and leave it on for 20 minutes. This mask pulls out impurities while calming inflammation.

Pro Tip: Freeze Aloe Vera gel in ice cube trays and use it to reduce puffiness around the eyes and calm skin redness instantly.
